What the pipeline does
  1. Chunks the corpus into 400-token passages
  2. Retrieves the top-5 passages relevant to your process
  3. Asks the LLM to extract every input & output flow
  4. Validates each flow against literature ranges
  5. Calculates GWP100 (IPCC AR6)
  6. Presents flagged flows for your review
  7. Exports JSON / CSV / ILCD XML after you approve
